Internet of Things (IoT) enables connecting devices, sensors, vehicles etc to collect and transfer data without human intervention. With IoT “things” or objects can be controlled remotely across existing network infrastructure. IoT enables integration of physical world into computer-based systems. Few of leading IoT solutions that are in the market are AWS IoT, Microsoft Azure, Thing Worx, IBM Watson IoT etc
